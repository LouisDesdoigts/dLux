    Fixed Sampling Propagator

    Bases: Propagator

    A propagator that samples the electric field in the output plane at the rate of one fringe per pixel where a fringe is a wavelength divided by the aperture diameter.

    These propagators are implemented using the numpy.fft sub-sub-package and cannot be modified elsewise.
